Introduction

Welcome to Anita Khuu and Carson Bain’s meta site for the final COMP1950 project. We have been tasked with coming up with a complete redesign of the current COMP1950 site. This meta site’s purpose is to provide background information and rationale on our new design as well as to supply a site map and navigation to the site itself.

Responsibilities

In terms of project responsibilities, Anita took the lead on designing and coding the HTML and CSS for the whole meta site including the site map. On the proto site, she coded and structured all the content on the Quiz page as well as the Resources page (HTML and CSS) and created all print styles. Carson was responsible for coding the header navigation, the main content container layout and the content for the Home and Lecture pages for the proto site. Both team members worked collaboratively on ideas for the overall site design, layout and colour scheme as well as intro/rationale content for the meta site.
